Privacy Policy

Kuizu (kuizuweb.netlify.app) is compliant with The General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). It means that we are open about our methods of tracking and use of the visitor's and personal data, and you can freely check what exactly we are doing to it. At Kuizu, we collect:

a. Your full name.
b. Your email address.
c. Your gender.
d. Your phone number.
e. The data about the browser and device you use to view the app.
f. The way you navigate the app.

We collect your contact details because they are necessary for your participation and interaction on the platform and for legal purposes. We gather the details of your technical equipment and on-site behaviour to make our platform more user-friendly and to personalize our services for you. Mass mailing services use your email address to send you emails (if you have subscribed for them) If you keep browsing our app/web app after reading this Privacy Policy, you give us the consent to use your details for the purposes explained above. If you do not agree with these terms, please do not use the platform. You can email us at hello@kuizu.xyz and ask:
a. To receive a copy of your personal details we have collected.
b. To delete your personal details from our system.
c. To withdraw your consent (if you previously agreed to provide us with the data, but then changed your mind).
We are doing our best to guarantee the security of your personal details while keeping and using them. Thank you for your cooperation!
